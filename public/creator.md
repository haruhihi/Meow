### 🌸 Spring's Gentle Whisper 🌸

In the heart of spring, where blossoms bloom, 🌷  
Nature awakens from winter's gloom. ❄️  
Birds sing sweetly, a melodious tune, 🎶  
Under the glow of the golden noon. ☀️

Cherry blossoms dance in the breeze, 🌸  
Petals fluttering with such ease. 🍃  
Green leaves sprout on every tree, 🌳  
A vibrant world for all to see. 🌈

Streams flow gently, crystal clear, 💧  
Whispering secrets for all to hear. 🐦  
Butterflies flutter, colors bright, 🦋  
A tapestry of pure delight. 🌼

Spring's embrace, so warm and kind, 🤗  
Brings joy and peace to heart and mind. 💖  
In every corner, life anew, 🌱  
Spring's gentle whisper, just for you. 🌷
